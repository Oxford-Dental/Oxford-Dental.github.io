About Us

We are here to serve Oxford and the surrounding area with general dental services. We are a team of people who truly love their work and are dedicated to serving our community and you. We love getting to know you, listening to your story, trying to understand what you need from us and how we can help you most.

We can provide you with urgent care and also general maintenance of your teeth and oral health. We welcome both teenagers using the free dental health scheme and younger children when referred by the school dental therapists. We can also help with ACC treatment and those who require funding help with WINZ.

We look forward to meeting you and answering your queries.
